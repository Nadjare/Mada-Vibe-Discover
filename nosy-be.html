Tourist Sites of Nosy Be

ISLAND PARADISE

Nosy Iranja

A paradise island connected by a sandbar at low tide. Perfect for romantic walks and turtle watching.

TURTLE ISLAND

Nosy Sakatia

Observation of land turtles and snorkeling in clear waters. An ecological and preserved site.

LEMUR ISLAND

Nosy Komba

Meet the famous macaque lemurs in freedom and discover local crafts in traditional villages.

MARINE RESERVE

Nosy Tanikely

Marine reserve protected with crystal clear waters ideal for snorkeling with tropical fish.

NATIONAL PARK

Lokobe National Park

The last primary humid forest of Nosy Be with unique endemic species and lemurs.

LEMUR PARK

Lemurian Land Park

Discovery of flora and lemurs in semi-freedom. An educational and family visit.

HISTORICAL

Marodoka Ghost House

Heritage and mystery from the colonial era. Discover the fascinating history of this legendary place.

PANORAMIC VIEW

Mont Passot

The highest point of the island offering a 360¬∞ panorama and its sacred lakes. Ideal for sunsets.
