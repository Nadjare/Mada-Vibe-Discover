Complete Site Details

The Three Bays

North coast of Diego-Suarez

Dunes Bay: Unique white sand dunes in the world plunging into the ocean. Ideal site for lounging, sunbathing and swimming in turquoise waters.

Pigeon Bay: Small intimate bay with rock formations sculpted by erosion. Paradise for snorkeling with great variety of tropical fish.

Ankarana Bay: Access to Ankarana reserve with its Tsingy, mysterious caves and underground rivers. Observation of lemurs and crocodiles.

  • Some of the most beautiful dunes in the world
  • Crystal clear waters for swimming
  • Exceptional snorkeling
  • Panoramic viewpoints

Red Tsingy

70km south of Diego-Suarez

Unique geological formation in the world: blood-red rocky peaks created by iron oxidation. Lunar and desert landscape that changes color depending on the time of day.

Red Tsingy are the result of millions of years of erosion. The iron-rich soil oxidized upon contact with air, giving this intense red color. The site is particularly spectacular at sunrise and sunset.

  • Unique red rock formations
  • Lunar desert landscape
  • Colors changing with sunlight
  • Exceptional geological site

Amber Mountain National Park

35km south of Diego-Suarez

Tropical rainforest with exceptional biodiversity: 7 species of lemurs, 75 species of birds, and unique flora. Cool and pleasant climate all year round.

This park houses spectacular waterfalls, crater lakes and preserved primary forest. Here you'll find the world's smallest chameleon, rare orchids and hundred-year-old trees.

  • 7 species of lemurs
  • Waterfalls and crater lakes
  • Exceptional tropical flora
  • Cool and pleasant climate

Emerald Sea

Diego-Suarez Bay

Lagoon with transparent emerald green waters, protected by a coral reef. Exceptional site for snorkeling and swimming in calm and shallow waters.

The seabed is populated by colorful corals, tropical fish, turtles and rays. The water rarely reaches more than 1.5m deep, making it an ideal site for the whole family.

  • Crystal clear emerald green waters
  • Exceptional snorkeling
  • Abundant marine life
  • Shallow and calm waters

French Mountain & Its Baobabs

Outskirts of Diego-Suarez

Historical site with breathtaking panoramic view of the bay. The mountain is home to a forest of endemic dwarf baobabs and well-preserved French fortifications.

The baobabs of French Mountain are unique: they are dwarf baobabs (Adansonia rubrostipa) that don't exceed 5m in height. The site also offers spectacular views of Sugar Loaf and the entire bay.

  • 360° panoramic view
  • Forest of endemic dwarf baobabs
  • Historical fortifications
  • Easy and accessible hike

Antagnavo Town

Diego-Suarez region

Authentic town offering a glimpse of Malagasy local life. Colorful markets, traditional crafts and warm hospitality of the inhabitants.

Antagnavo is renowned for its sacred lake and ancestral traditions. It's the ideal place to discover Sakalava culture, taste local cuisine and buy artisanal souvenirs.

  • Sacred Lake of Antagnavo
  • Traditional markets
  • Authentic local crafts
  • Meeting with the population
