Destinations

Discover Madagascar's most beautiful locations. From northern beaches to southern deserts, each destination offers unique attractions with expert local guidance.

Antsiranana (Diego Suarez)

Natural harbor, colonial architecture, and access to Emerald Sea and Montagne d'Ambre National Park.

Nosy Be - The Perfumed Island

Beautiful beaches, ylang-ylang plantations, and marine life. Perfect for snorkeling, diving, and visiting nearby islands.

Ambanja & Sambirano Valley

Lush cocoa and coffee plantations in the fertile Sambirano Valley. Discover agricultural traditions.

R√©gion SAVA

Sambava, Antalaha, Voh√©mar, Andapa - Vanilla capital and beautiful landscapes of northeastern Madagascar.

Tamatave (Toamasina)

Main port city, beautiful beaches, and gateway to Pangalanes Canal and Ivoloina Park.

Antananarivo

Capital city with rich history and culture. Royal palaces, traditional markets, and day trips to national parks.

Antsirabe

Thermal springs, crater lakes, and rickshaw tours. Known for semi-precious stones and handicrafts.

Majunga (Mahajanga)

Beautiful baobab-lined avenue, beachfront promenade, and gateway to Ankarafantsika National Park.

Tulear (Toliara)

Gateway to the spiny forest, Ifaty coral reefs, and unique Vezo culture with traditional pirogues.

Morondava

Discover the famous Avenue of Baobabs, gateway to Tsingy de Bemaraha, and authentic Sakalava culture.

Sainte-Marie Island

Tropical paradise for whale watching (July-September), pristine beaches, and historical pirate legacy.

Fort Dauphin (Taolagnaro)

Beautiful beaches, Berenty Reserve for lemurs, and stunning mountain landscapes of the Anosy region.
